### How we work

Three ways, depending on how far you want us in:

- **Agency.** You own the brand and the vision. We supply the senior team to build it, under your name or ours.
- **Products.** You have a problem worth solving. We design and ship the whole product end to end, from zero to live.
- **Ventures.** For the right teams, we go further and join as technical co-founder, with a stake and skin in the game.

Fifteen products shipped across the UK, Europe, the US and MENA, spanning proptech, healthtech, sports, consumer and AI.

### How we build

- **Web & CMS:** WordPress, Etch, Cwicly, Contact Form 7
- **Backend:** Laravel, PHP, Filament
- **Mobile:** Flutter & Dart, NativeScript
- **Frontend:** React, TypeScript
- **Cloud & infrastructure:** AWS, DigitalOcean, Laravel Forge
- **Data & analytics:** Firebase, BigQuery
